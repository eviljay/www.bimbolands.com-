The Prime Bimbo is the democratically elected head of the humanist republic of bimbolands.

All Bimbo senators are eligible to put themselves up for nomination to become the Prime Bimbo of the humanist republic of Bimbolands.

What is the treasury?

How is the treasury calculated?
The treasury is funded by bimbo citizens.
